The Glory of Pink Porsches

By a loving admirer of pink

In rosy dreams of endless summer days,
A Porsche in pink, a sight that holds my gaze.
Its glossy curves, a tale of beauty told,
A vision in pink, a wonder to behold.

The sun's warm kiss upon its blushing hue,
A marvel crafted for the chosen few.
On winding roads, it glides with graceful ease,
A symphony of speed that aims to please.

Through city streets and down the open way,
It captures hearts and makes the spirits sway.
A masterpiece in pink, both bold and bright,
It turns the day to magic, pure delight.

So here’s to pink, the Porsche in its prime,
A testament to art, surpassing time.
